About me

Hi I'm

Raifa!

I'm a crocheter based in Beirut. I've been crocheting for around 7 years, needless to day my collection of yarn is getting out of hand!
When I'm not crocheting, I unravel wool sweaters to make my own yarn, bake cinnamon rolls using my Swedish great aunt's secret recipe, and worry about my dog's numerous allergies.
I also love going to the beach and discovering beautiful spots accross Lebanon.
